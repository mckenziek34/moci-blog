About Me

I’m Mckenzie, an undergraduate student and creative living in Detroit. I got the idea for ‘MoCi’ (Motor City) when I was considering how Detroit businesses are relevant to the cultural fabric of Detroit. I realized that I wanted to start a project while living in the city. My goal with the MoCi blog is to promote and advocate for artistic and individual expression in Detroit – especially when creating and promoting small businesses in the city. I plan to showcase my experiences in Detroit along with personal and commercial art projects – including the processes behind them. I also plan to create digital galleries for sketches, random photos, event experiences, and more!

I’m interested in everything. I’m interested in IT/technology in general, art, music, and making clothing! I also love video games and game design – another interest I'll explore further on this blog. As a project based in Detroit, I’ll share a lot of the imagery that inspires my creations. I’ll also talk about plans for Detroit and even share about decisions I think would benefit the city.
